Moving from Tactical to Strategic

We know the current CLM ecosystem at YETI feels like a "library" rather than a true workflow engine. Here is how DocuSign IAM bridges the gap between what you have and what an enterprise needs.

The Download/Upload Cycle

Currently, redlining requires downloading documents, editing locally, and re-uploading them. It's prone to version control issues.

Agreement Desk allows concurrent, native collaboration and redlining in a single, secure environment.

"Rudimentary" AI

Basic machine learning that routes documents or relies on simple questionnaires isn't true AI.

Navigator uses advanced Generative AI to deeply analyze international governing laws, auto-renewals, and terms across your entire repository instantly.

Rigid Routing Rules

Simple API calls that trigger at the end of a process limit visibility and don't account for complex, dynamic organizational structures.

Maestro offers a no-code workflow builder. Automate complex logic (e.g., threshold-based routing to FP&A) seamlessly.

DocuSign Navigator

Stop treating contracts like flat PDFs. Navigator transforms your agreements into structured data. With YETI operating in nearly 40 countries, Navigator allows you to instantly query your repository: "Return every agreement where the governing law is in California" or "Show me all vendor renewals requiring 60-day notice in the EU."

  • Automated reporting and dashboarding
  • Intelligent clause extraction
  • Multi-language support for global operations

Maestro & Agreement Desk

Say goodbye to the "download, redline, re-upload" dance. Agreement Desk provides a centralized workspace for negotiation, empowered by AI-assisted review to instantly flag third-party risks against YETI's legal playbooks and suggest pre-approved fallback language. Pair it with Maestro to build logic-based workflows (like routing to specific FP&A contacts based on spend) without writing a single line of code.

  • AI-assisted risk detection and automated redlining
  • End-to-end process visibility
  • Real-time, concurrent collaboration
